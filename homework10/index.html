Interactive Line Chart

To track the COVID-19 vaccination in the World, this line chart displays the top five countries with the total number of vaccinations from December 2020 to September 2021.

The total number of vaccinations is the absolute number of total immunizations in the country. It is an accumulated number.

By clicking the country name on the legend, you can see the tendency of the total vaccinations for the country.
